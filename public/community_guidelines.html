4. Your content is your responsibility: Any content that you upload, post, publish, or email through Khul Ke is your responsibility. Please make sure that you have the right to share it.

6. Be mindful of your posts: We don’t allow any inappropriate content on Khul Ke, including nudity, profanity, and violence. We also don’t allow content that promotes terrorism, hate groups, or the buying and selling of firearms, alcohol, and tobacco.
7. Think before you post: Please don’t share anything that could harm anyone else or infringe on anyone’s rights. If you’re not sure whether something is appropriate, it’s probably best not to post it.
Remember, Khul Ke is a community of real people. So please be yourself and have fun!
We want to make sure that our Community Guidelines protect people from harmful content and any types of abuse.
