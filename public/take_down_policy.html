TAKE DOWN POLICY

To report any Content on our platforms, please follow the following steps:
1) Navigate to the Content you’d like to report.
2) Tap the icon [●] located at the top of the Content.
3) Select Report Content.
4) If you select It's abusive or harmful, we’ll ask you to provide additional information about the issue you’re reporting.
5) We will include the reported Content in our follow-up emails and notifications to you. To opt-out of receiving this information, please uncheck the box next to Updates about this report can show reported Content.
6) Once you’ve submitted your report, we’ll decide on whether the Content should be taken down.
7) We will also provide recommendations for additional actions you can take to improve your experience of use of our Services.
